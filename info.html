Madison VonNeida's Infographic

                           


















This project required me to investigate communication technology in the form of an infographic. An infographic is a visual representation of information which presents complex information quickly and clearly.

The communication technology I chose is speakers. I used howstuffworks.com and explainhatstuff.com to find the required fields to complete this project. The infographic was created on InDesign, at 17 x 11. I then printed the infographic for completion.

By clicking the infographic, it will zoom in to get a closer look!
